Auto Ads

Turn on Auto ads in your Google AdSense dashboard. Google's AI finds the best extra positions on your page and inserts ads automatically — this alone can increase revenue by 20–40% on top of your manual slots. No extra code needed here once you enable it in AdSense.

Tips to maximise revenue

1. Enable "Anchor ads" in AdSense — a sticky banner that floats at the bottom of mobile screens. These have very high CTR on resume builders.

2. Enable "Vignette ads" — full-screen interstitials that show between page views. Google controls frequency so they're not annoying.

3. Category blocking — in AdSense go to Brand safety → Content → Blocking controls. Block low-paying categories (dating, gambling) to push CPM up.

4. Experiment with ad placement — use Google's built-in A/B testing in AdSense to find which slot positions earn most.

5. Add a Privacy Policy page — AdSense requires one and it unlocks personalised ads which pay 3–5× more than contextual-only.
